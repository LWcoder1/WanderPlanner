Our Mission

Welcome to Wander Planner, your go-to travel itinerary app designed to help you discover and plan your perfect trips. Our team uses the Four Square API to bring you tailored travel recommendations.

By inputting coordinates, radius, and specific queries, our app fetches a curated list of locations to create personalized travel plans that match your interests and schedule. We optimize API calls for better performance and cost-efficiency, ensuring a smooth planning experience.

Our website offers a user-friendly interface with features like an automatic image slideshow and easy navigation. Explore destinations with ease and enjoy a visually appealing, functional experience. At Wander Planner, we’re committed to improving design, API efficiency, and features, providing you with the best tools for your next adventure. Thank you for choosing Wander Planner – your journey starts here!
